🔍 Exploring Insertion Sort 🔍

Welcome to the world of sorting algorithms! Let's dive into the concept of Insertion Sort. 🚀

What is Insertion Sort? 🧩

Insertion Sort is a simple sorting algorithm that builds the final sorted array one element at a time. It's like sorting a deck of cards in your hand!

How does it work? 🔄

It works by iterating through the array, comparing elements, and moving them into their correct position. 📊
